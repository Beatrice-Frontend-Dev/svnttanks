Our Facilities

Overview of Our Facilities

Gas Gathering Facility

This facility collects raw natural gas from multiple wells, consolidates it, and removes basic impurities like water and debris. This stage ensures the gas is ready for safe transport to the next processing step.

Central Processing Facility

Here, the raw gas undergoes detailed treatment, including removing contaminants like sulfur and carbon dioxide, dehydration, and separating valuable components like natural gas liquids.

Tank Farm

Finally, the refined products are stored in a tank farm, ensuring safe storage, effective distribution, and readiness for export or market supply.

Our Commitments:

Health, Safety, Environment, and Quality

Health

Svnt Tanks Terminal takes full responsibility for preserving the quality of its clients' products. Each is handled according to the highest standards. Svnt Tanks Terminal ensures compliance with all the requirements regarding the safe handling of products. The principles of Svnt Tanks Terminal health policy apply to every employee and every Svnt Tanks Terminal location and vessel. They clearly define priorities and roles. The main goal of the policy is to build consensus and to inform workers about safe and healthy working methods.

Safety

At Svnt Tanks, safety is paramount. We prioritize the well-being of our employees, the protection of the environment, and the security of the valuable gas products entrusted to our care. We adhere to the highest industry safety standards and rigorously comply with all relevant regulations, including those pertaining to the handling and storage of flammable and explosive materials. Svnt Tanks fosters a strong safety culture, implementing comprehensive safety policies and procedures that cover all aspects of our operations, from the receipt and storage of gas to its delivery and offloading.

Environment

At Svnt Tanks, we are committed to minimizing our environmental impact and operating in an environmentally responsible manner. We adhere to all applicable environmental regulations and strive to continuously improve our environmental performance. We implement best practices to prevent pollution and minimize emissions, such as utilizing advanced vapor recovery systems and minimizing fugitive emissions. We promote the responsible use of resources, such as energy and water, by implementing energy-efficient equipment and utilizing rainwater harvesting systems.

Quality

At Svnt Tanks, we are committed to delivering the highest quality service and ensuring the integrity of our clients' gas products. We maintain rigorous quality control measures throughout our operations, from the initial receipt and inspection of gas products to their final delivery. Our state-of-the-art terminals are equipped with advanced systems for monitoring and controlling product quality, ensuring the accurate measurement and segregation of different gas grades. We employ a highly skilled team of professionals with expertise in gas handling, storage, and logistics.
